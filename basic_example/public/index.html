Welcome

to

iDipity!

Welcome to iDipity (ideas+serendipity), a web service that facilitates the serendipitous meeting of ideas! We believe that great ideas are born, not in solitude but from the serendipity that arises from the beautiful interaction of people and thoughts known as "conversation."

iDipity is a platform for discovering and sharing ideas through intimate and serendipitous online video conversations at virtual "tables" in virtual "cafés" with other users from all around the world!

What you will find on this site is a very early prototype of the product we one day want to develop into a global platform for discussing ideas. We would greatly appreciate any constructive feedback that will help us realize this goal.

So pop into a café, have a seat at a table and join the conversation!

And welcome to iDipity, where ideas and minds can meet!

-The iDipity Team
